LITTLE MIX

Who are Little Mix?

Little mix are a British girl band formed in 2011 during the eighth series of the British version of the X Factor.Interestingly, they won the competition which led to their rise of fame. The group consists os Jade Thirwall, Perrie Edwards and Leigh-Anne Pinnock. A former member Jesy Nelson departed the group in December 2020. The group are well known for their strong vocals and signature harmonies as well as their representative songs about female empowerment and their social activism including LGBT rights. They have established themselves as the biggest girl band of the the 2010s, selling over 60 million records worldwide throughout their career making them one of the bext selling girl groups of all time.

  4. Perrie Edwards
